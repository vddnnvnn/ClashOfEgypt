    How It Works

    Tournament Rules

    01
    Registration
    Register online or in-person. Confirm your spot with a 50 EGP deposit. Full refund if cancelled 48hrs in advance.
    02
    Check-In
    Arrive 30 minutes before tournament start. Bring valid ID. Late arrivals may forfeit their bracket position.
    03
    Match Format
    Single or double elimination depending on bracket size. Best of 3 matches, Grand Finals best of 5.
    04
    Fair Play
    Zero tolerance for cheating, hacking, or toxic behavior. Violations result in immediate disqualification.
    05
    Prize Distribution
    1st: 50% | 2nd: 30% | 3rd: 20% of prize pool. Prizes paid within 24 hours of tournament end.
    06
    Equipment
    All equipment provided by Clash of Egypt. Personal peripherals (mouse, keyboard) allowed if declared.

    Who We Are

    Egypt's Premier Gaming Destination

    Clash of Egypt was born from a vision: to create a world-class gaming arena that honors Egypt's legacy of greatness while embracing the future of digital entertainment. Located in the heart of the New Administrative Capital, we are the country's most advanced esports and gaming facility.

    We believe gaming is more than entertainment — it's a culture, a community, and a competitive sport. Our mission is to elevate the Egyptian gaming scene to global standards, nurture local talent, and create unforgettable experiences for every player who walks through our doors.

    Our Journey

    The Clash Story

    2023
    The Vision
    Clash of Egypt was conceptualized by a group of passionate gamers and entrepreneurs who saw a gap in Egypt's entertainment landscape.
    2024
    Grand Opening
    We opened our doors in the New Administrative Capital with 30 stations and hosted our first tournament with over 200 participants.
    2024
    VR Zone Launch
    Expanded with Egypt's largest commercial VR zone, featuring 10 premium headsets and immersive experiences across 30+ titles.
    2025
    Championship Series
    Launched the Clash Championship Series — Egypt's premier esports league with partnerships with international gaming brands.
